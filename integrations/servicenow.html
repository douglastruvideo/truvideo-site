ServiceNow Video Integration

Add video documentation, visual proof of work, and AI-powered inspection to every ServiceNow Field Service Management work order. TruVideo's Video SDK connects directly to your ServiceNow instance, giving field technicians the tools to capture, annotate, and share video on every job.

Talk to Our Integration Team → Explore the Video SDK

How It Works

Video-enriched work orders in four steps

Connect

Authenticate TruVideo with your ServiceNow instance via OAuth 2.0. Configure work order types and assignment groups for video capture.

Capture

Field technicians record video directly from the ServiceNow mobile app or TruVideo's SDK-powered capture. GPS, timestamps, and work order IDs are embedded automatically.

Analyze

TruVideo's AI engine processes video in real time — detecting damage, verifying completion, and extracting structured data from visual inspections.

Attach & Close

Videos, AI summaries, and proof-of-work documentation attach directly to the ServiceNow work order. Dispatchers and customers see results instantly.

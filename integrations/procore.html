Procore Video Integration

Add video documentation to every Procore construction project. Site inspections, daily reports, subcontractor verification, and punch lists — all captured with video proof and powered by TruVideo's AI. Stop relying on photos that miss context and written notes that miss details.

How It Works

Video-documented construction in four steps

Connect Projects

Link TruVideo to your Procore account via OAuth. Select which projects, trades, and inspection types should include video documentation.

Record on Site

Field teams capture video walkthroughs using smartphones or TruLabs smart glasses. Videos are tagged with project, location, and inspection type automatically.

AI Analysis

TruVideo's computer vision models analyze footage for safety hazards, work quality issues, and completion status. Structured findings are generated in seconds.

Sync to Procore

Videos, AI summaries, and inspection findings sync directly to the corresponding Procore project — attached to daily logs, observations, or punch list items.

Key Capabilities

Video proof for every phase of construction

🏗

Project-Linked Video

Every video is automatically associated with the correct Procore project, location, and trade. No manual tagging, no misplaced footage, no ambiguity about which site a video belongs to.

🔎

Inspection Documentation

Replace static photo checklists with video walkthroughs. AI extracts findings from footage so inspectors can focus on the work, not paperwork.

📋

Daily Report Enrichment

Attach time-stamped video to daily logs. Project managers and owners see exactly what happened on site, not just what was written in a report.

☑

Punch List Video

Document deficiencies with video that shows exactly what needs to be fixed, where, and why. Subcontractors get clear visual instructions instead of ambiguous text descriptions.

⚠

Safety Compliance

AI detects PPE violations, fall hazards, and unsafe conditions in real time. Video evidence supports OSHA documentation and safety audit requirements.

👥

Subcontractor Verification

Video proof of work from subcontractors before payment is released. Timestamped, geolocated footage eliminates disputes about what was completed and when.

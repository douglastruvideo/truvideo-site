TruVideo for OEMs

How OEMs use TruVideo to gain visibility, standardize quality, and improve warranty accuracy across their dealer and service networks.

The Visibility Gap

You invest millions in product quality.
Then lose visibility at the factory gate.

The moment a vehicle, machine, or piece of equipment leaves your factory, you lose direct control over how it's inspected, serviced, and represented to customers. Dealer inspections are inconsistent. Warranty claims lack evidence. Product defects take months to surface. Video is the missing data layer.

Inconsistent dealer inspections

Every dealer does PDIs and service inspections differently. Some use checklists, some use photos, some use nothing. You have no way to verify that your inspection protocol was actually followed — or that the inspection even happened. Quality standards exist on paper but not in practice.

Most OEMs cannot verify inspection compliance across their dealer network

Warranty claims without evidence

Dealers submit warranty claims with text descriptions and sometimes a photo. Was the damage pre-existing? Did it happen during transport? Was the repair actually necessary? Without timestamped video evidence, every claim is a judgment call — and billions in warranty spend goes unverified.

Warranty fraud and misclassification cost OEMs billions annually

Slow product feedback loops

A recurring defect shows up across multiple dealers. But field reports are text-based, inconsistently filed, and disconnected from engineering systems. By the time the pattern is visible, thousands of units have shipped. Visual evidence would have surfaced the trend months earlier.

Average time from field defect to engineering awareness: 3-6 months

Brand experience varies by location

Your brand promise depends on dealer execution. But you have limited visibility into how service is actually delivered across hundreds or thousands of locations. Customer experience varies wildly. The dealers who need the most support are the ones you hear from least.

Service quality is the #1 driver of brand loyalty post-purchase

How It Works

Three steps from raw video
to OEM intelligence.

TruVideo embeds into your existing dealer tools and service workflows. No new hardware. No separate app for your network to adopt. Video becomes a data layer inside the systems your dealers already use.

1

Capture

Video SDK embedded in dealer and service tools

Our Video SDK integrates directly into DMS platforms, service applications, and mobile tools your dealers already use. Guided capture workflows ensure consistent, high-quality video at every inspection point. Chunked resumable uploads handle bad connectivity in service bays and field environments. Works on any iOS or Android device.

2

Analyze

AI processes every frame, extracts structured data

Computer vision models trained on 80M+ real-world service videos analyze every frame. Damage detection, parts identification, condition grading, and inspection completeness — all extracted automatically. Raw video becomes structured, queryable data tagged to specific assets, locations, and events.

3

Act

Dashboards, alerts, and integrations with OEM systems

Network-wide dashboards surface trends, anomalies, and compliance metrics. Automated alerts flag warranty claims that need review, locations falling below inspection standards, or emerging product quality patterns. Data flows into your warranty management, quality management, and CRM systems via API.

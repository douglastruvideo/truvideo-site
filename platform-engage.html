Capabilities

What Engage does

calendar_month

Service Appointment Scheduling

Engage handles the full appointment booking flow across web chat, SMS, and phone — checking availability, confirming service needs, and writing the appointment to your DMS. No human required for standard scheduling.

forum

Conversational Web & Chat

Deploys on your dealership website or service portal as a conversational agent. Answers service FAQs, captures vehicle information, qualifies intent, and books appointments — 24/7 without staffing cost.

call

Phone & Voice Interactions

Handles inbound service calls using modern voice AI models. Understands natural speech, captures intent, and either resolves the request or routes to a human advisor — with full context passed on handoff.

support_agent

Intelligent Escalation

When a conversation exceeds Engage's confidence threshold, it escalates to a human — with the full conversation history, customer record, and recommended next action. Humans take over informed, not cold.

DMS & CRM integration

Engage reads and writes to CDK Global, DealerSocket, Tekion, and Reynolds & Reynolds. Appointments land in the schedule. Customer records update automatically. No double-entry.

Rebuilding for cross-vertical usage

Engage is evolving from its automotive roots into a broader service orchestration layer. The same conversational intelligence that books a vehicle service appointment is being extended to field service dispatch, fleet maintenance scheduling, and MRO work order initiation.

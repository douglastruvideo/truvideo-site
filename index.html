Platform

Three layers. One platform.
Infinite applications.

Most video tools stop at capture. TruVideo is a full-stack visual intelligence platform — Capture, Analyze, Act — designed for enterprises that need to prove what happened, when it happened, and what to do next.

Capture

Mobile SDK, web recorder, AR glasses, and Send2Me — every input channel feeds the same intelligence pipeline. BYOAI and BYOS architecture means your data stays yours.

Analyze

Video Query Service (VQS) extracts structured data from video in real time. AI models detect damage, classify conditions, generate inspection reports — no human review needed.

Act

Intelligent messaging delivers findings to the right person at the right time. Compliance workflows, approval chains, and automated alerts — video becomes action.

How it works

Three steps. Zero friction.

send

1. Send a Link

Generate a capture link from your DMS, CRM, or service portal. Send via SMS, email, or in-app message. The link opens a guided capture experience in the customer's mobile browser — no App Store, no Play Store, no login.

videocam

2. Customer Captures

Guided prompts walk the customer through the capture — vehicle walk-around, damage close-ups, odometer, VIN plate. Capture quality is validated in real time. Poor lighting, motion blur, and coverage gaps trigger automatic re-capture prompts.

inbox

3. You Receive Structured Data

Video and images arrive in your platform with metadata — timestamp, GPS, capture sequence, and VQS quality scores. The submission links to the customer record and triggers your workflow automatically.

Applications

Where Intake changes the workflow

directions_car

Trade-In Appraisal

Customers submit vehicle condition before arriving at the dealership. Appraisers work from structured evidence, not a cold walk-around. Deals move faster because information arrives first.

gavel

Remote Claims Submission

Policyholders capture damage evidence at the scene or at home. Adjusters receive timestamped, geotagged video — not low-quality photos texted from a personal phone.

check_circle

Pre-Delivery Inspection

Document asset condition before it leaves your facility. Customer captures condition at delivery. Both records create a before/after baseline that eliminates delivery damage disputes.

build

Maintenance Requests

Field teams or tenants submit visual evidence of issues without a site visit. Dispatchers triage from video before rolling a truck.

key

Lease Return

Lessees document vehicle condition before return. Condition records are timestamped and geotagged — protecting both parties and accelerating the return process.

engineering

Field Service Intake

Customers or site contacts initiate service requests with visual evidence. Field service managers see the issue before dispatching a technician — reducing unnecessary truck rolls.

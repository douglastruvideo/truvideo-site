Supported Platforms

Every major glasses platform, natively integrated

Each platform has different strengths. TruLabs integrates them all so you can deploy the right tool for the right context.

XREAL
One Series · Eye · Beam Pro · Project Aura
AR Display 12MP Camera Android XR

The world's largest AR glasses platform by units shipped. XREAL One + Eye module delivers 12MP first-person video capture with a 57° see-through display — AI overlays, spatial annotations, and hands-free documentation in one device.

Project Aura brings XREAL into the Android XR ecosystem with a 70°+ field of view, Gemini AI integration, and full Google Play access. Best-in-class for complex spatial documentation, maintenance overlays, and mixed reality inspection records.

Spectacles
Snap 5th Gen · Snap OS 2.0
On-Device AI Multi-User AR SnapML

Snap's fully untethered AR glasses run SnapML — custom machine learning models that execute directly on the camera feed. Run TruVideo's defect detection models on-device, flag anomalies in real time without a cloud round-trip.

Connected Lenses enables multiple Spectacles users to share a synchronized AR workspace simultaneously — ideal for team inspections where multiple technicians need to see the same AR overlays on the same physical asset.

Meta Ray-Ban
Ray-Ban Meta · Oakley Meta
1M+ Deployed Live Streaming Meta AI

Over one million deployed. Meta Ray-Ban glasses look identical to regular sunglasses — zero social friction in the field. Workers wear them without resistance. The 12MP camera captures 1080p/60fps video; Meta AI provides real-time "look and tell" intelligence through the open-ear speaker.

TruVideo integrates at the live stream layer — the glasses stream first-person video into TruVideo's AI pipeline for real-time analysis, structured data extraction, and expert routing. 4+ hour battery life makes them practical for full-shift deployment.

Android XR
Google · Samsung · XREAL Aura
Open Platform Gemini AI Enterprise MDM

Google's open XR platform runs across headsets, wired glasses, and AI glasses from a single Jetpack XR SDK. Gemini AI is baked in at the OS level — real-time visual understanding of anything the camera sees, with voice response through the earpiece.

Android XR's open ecosystem model means enterprises can deploy via existing Android MDM infrastructure, distribute apps through Google Play, and leverage standard Android Enterprise security. One codebase runs across headsets, wired glasses, and AI glasses — adapting its UI at runtime based on the device.

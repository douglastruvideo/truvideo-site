The Problem

Your team loses 15+ hours/week
to meetings that should be async.

Code reviews. Bug reports. Architecture walkthroughs. Sprint demos. Onboarding. They're all better as recorded context than scheduled synchronous interruptions that fragment everyone's flow state.

And when you do use a screen recorder, it probably stores your proprietary code walkthroughs on someone else's servers. That's a security team's nightmare.

How It Works

Three steps. Zero meetings.

1

Record

One click from the browser extension or desktop app. Screen, camera, or both. Draw, annotate, and highlight as you narrate. No "can you see my screen?" — just hit record.

2

AI processes it instantly

VQS generates a full transcript, summary, and chapter markers the moment you stop recording. Recipients jump straight to the 30-second segment that matters — no scrubbing through 20 minutes for 2 minutes of context.

3

Share a link

Paste the link in Slack, attach it to a Jira ticket, or embed it in Confluence. Viewers comment on specific timestamps. The async conversation happens when people have time — not when Calendly says so.

FAQ

Questions answered

How is TruCast different from Loom?
TruCast is built for engineering teams with enterprise security requirements. The core differentiator is BYOS — your recordings live in your cloud storage, not ours. We also offer deeper integrations with developer tools (Jira, GitHub, Linear), self-hosted deployment options, BYOAI processing, and audit logs with SIEM export. Loom is great for sales and marketing. TruCast is purpose-built for engineering.
What does BYOS mean exactly?
Bring Your Own Storage. When you record a TruCast video, the recording streams directly to your cloud storage bucket (AWS S3, Azure Blob Storage, or Google Cloud Storage). The video data never touches TruVideo servers. We handle the recording logic, AI processing pipeline, and playback CDN — but your actual video files stay in infrastructure you own and control.
Can I use TruCast without BYOS?
Yes. The Starter plan (free) includes 5 GB of TruVideo-managed cloud storage. BYOS is available on Team and Enterprise plans for teams that need data sovereignty. You can start free and migrate to BYOS when your security team requires it — no re-recording needed.
What AI model processes my recordings?
TruCast uses TruVideo's Video Query Service (VQS) for transcription, summarization, and chapter generation. On the Enterprise plan, BYOAI lets you plug in your own AI models — run everything through your Azure OpenAI instance, your self-hosted Whisper deployment, or any model endpoint. The AI pipeline is fully configurable.
Is there a recording length limit?
No. All plans support unlimited recording length. We don't charge by the minute. The Starter plan limits the number of recordings (25/month); Team and Enterprise are unlimited in every dimension.
Can viewers watch without creating an account?
Yes. Public and link-only recordings can be viewed by anyone with the URL — no account required. For team-restricted recordings, viewers authenticate through your SSO provider. You control the access model per recording or per workspace.
What platforms does the recorder support?
Chrome extension (works on any Chromium browser), macOS desktop app, and Windows desktop app. The desktop apps support system audio capture and multi-monitor recording. Linux support is on the roadmap.
How does the self-hosted option work?
Enterprise plan includes a Docker/Kubernetes deployment package. The entire TruCast stack — recording ingest, AI processing, playback CDN, and web UI — runs in your VPC. Nothing leaves your network. We provide Helm charts, Terraform modules, and dedicated onboarding support.

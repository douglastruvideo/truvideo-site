FAQ

Questions answered

Is the free plan actually free forever?
Yes. Unlimited recordings, unlimited length, no seat counting. You pay for your own S3 storage and AI API calls directly to those providers. TruVideo charges nothing for the free tier. No credit card required. No auto-upgrade. No sleeper seat billing.
How does auto-recording work?
TruVideo detects when you join a Zoom, Google Meet, Microsoft Teams, or Slack Huddle. Recording starts automatically. When the call ends, the recording is saved to your storage, transcribed by your AI, and action items are extracted into Jira tickets. You can disable auto-recording per app or per meeting.
What does BYOS mean exactly?
Bring Your Own Storage. When you record, the file streams directly to your cloud storage bucket (AWS S3, Azure Blob Storage, or Google Cloud Storage). The video data never touches TruVideo servers. We handle the recording logic, AI pipeline, and playback — but your actual files stay in infrastructure you own and control.
Can I use it without connecting my own storage?
Yes. You can use TruVideo-managed storage temporarily while you set up your own bucket. But BYOS is how TruVideo was designed to work — and it's available on every plan, including free.
How does Jira ticket generation work?
After each meeting, your AI model transcribes the call and identifies action items, decisions, and owners. TruVideo creates Jira tickets automatically, linked to the correct epic if one exists. You review and confirm before they're published, or set it to auto-create if you trust the workflow.
What platforms does TruVideo support?
macOS desktop app is available now. Windows is in beta. The app works with Zoom, Google Meet, Microsoft Teams, and Slack Huddles. Browser-based screen recording is also available via Chrome extension.
What about my existing Loom recordings?
Your old Loom links keep working — that's Loom's problem to maintain. TruVideo doesn't need an import. Just start recording. New meetings go to your infrastructure from day one.
Is TruVideo new? Can I trust it?
TruVideo was built after Atlassian broke Loom — by the team behind the TruVideo visual intelligence platform, which processes 80M+ videos across 3,500+ business locations. The recording infrastructure is proven. The product is new because the problem is new.

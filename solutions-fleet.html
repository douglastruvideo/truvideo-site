Asset Memory

Every vehicle builds its own condition history

Paper DVIRs create compliance records. TruVideo creates asset memory — a persistent, measured visual record that follows each vehicle across every driver, every route, every inspection. When damage appears, you know exactly when it happened and who had the vehicle.

Damage attribution, not just documentation

Pre/post-trip comparison with AR overlays means new damage is immediately visible against the vehicle's spatial record. Disputes about who caused damage get resolved with video evidence, not conversations. Recoverable costs that used to be written off become recoverable again.

80M+ real-world inspection videos

TruVideo's AI was trained on over 80 million real service and inspection videos — including commercial vehicles, varied lighting, one-handed capture, and noisy environments. Models that understand fleet conditions, not just controlled test environments.

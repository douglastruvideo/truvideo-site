Architecture

Two products. One spatial intelligence platform.

TruVideo Inspect ships as two distinct products depending on the asset type.

directions_car

Conditional Reporting

Damage attribution on defined physical assets — vehicles, aircraft, equipment. Technicians capture a mobile video walk-around; TruVideo generates a 3D mesh anchored to the asset. Re-inspection projects previously documented damage as AR overlays, making new damage instantly visible. Every inspection creates a timestamped spatial record.

  • Automotive trade-in & loaner
  • Aircraft ground damage
  • Fleet vehicle handoffs
  • Lease-end condition documentation
domain

FacilityEye

Environment-level change detection without pre-defined asset models. FacilityEye detects what's missing, what's new, and what's changed in a space — without requiring a 3D model to exist beforehand. Built for spaces that evolve over time, not assets with fixed geometry.

  • Server rooms & data centers
  • Telecom towers & cabinets
  • Building interiors
  • Construction site progress
Capabilities

What makes Inspect impossible to replicate

view_in_ar

3D Mesh Generation

Mobile video walk-around generates a calibrated 3D spatial mesh of the asset. No fixed hardware. No $50K camera tunnel. A technician's phone does in 90 seconds what competing systems require specialized equipment to achieve.

memory

Persistent Asset Memory

The mesh is saved, tagged, and reloaded across every future inspection. The asset remembers everything that's been documented — building a permanent condition history from the first inspection forward.

layers

AR Overlay Projection

During re-inspection, previously documented damage is projected as AR overlays onto the live camera view. New damage is immediately visible against the spatial record — eliminating "he said/she said" disputes.

compare

Change-of-State Tracking

Every inspection is compared against the previous spatial record. Changes are flagged, measured, and timestamped — creating a verifiable evidence chain across custody events.

mic

Voice-First Capture

Technicians narrate during walk-arounds; speech-to-text converts voice to structured inspection data. No typing. No forms. No friction in the service bay.

auto_awesome

AI Auto-Detection

Local AI models identify damage during capture — scratches, dents, cracks, fluid leaks. Auto-detection surfaces findings for technician confirmation, accelerating inspection without removing human judgment.

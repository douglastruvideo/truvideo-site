Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

TruVideo, Inc. ("TruVideo," "we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting the privacy and security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, disclose, and protect information when you use our visual intelligence platform, websites, mobile applications, and related services (collectively, the "Services").

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, including: name, email address, company name, job title, phone number, and any other information you choose to provide when creating an account, requesting a demo, or contacting us.

When you use our Services, we automatically collect certain information, including: device information, IP addresses, browser type, usage data, and video metadata (timestamps, geolocation, device identifiers). Note that video content captured through our platform is processed according to the terms of our customer agreements and is stored in accordance with our BYOS (Bring Your Own Storage) architecture where applicable.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to: provide, maintain, and improve our Services; process and respond to your requests; send you technical notices, security alerts, and support messages; communicate with you about products, services, and events; monitor and analyze trends, usage, and activities; detect, investigate, and prevent security incidents; and comply with legal obligations.

3. Video Data and BYOS Architecture

TruVideo's platform processes video data on behalf of our customers. Under our BYOS (Bring Your Own Storage) architecture, video content may be stored in the customer's own cloud infrastructure (AWS, Azure, GCP, or on-premises). TruVideo processes video data to provide our visual intelligence services, including AI analysis, damage detection, condition scoring, and structured data extraction. We do not sell video data or use it for purposes other than providing our contracted services.

4. Information Sharing

We may share your information with: service providers who perform services on our behalf; business partners and integration partners as directed by our customers; in response to legal process or government requests; to protect the rights, property, and safety of TruVideo, our customers, or others; and in connection with any merger, sale, or acquisition.

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. Our platform is SOC 2 compliant, and we employ encryption in transit and at rest, role-based access controls, and comprehensive audit logging.

6. Data Retention

We retain personal information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected, comply with our legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements. Video data retention is governed by individual customer agreements and the customer's own retention policies under BYOS deployments.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal information, including the right to access, correct, delete, port, or restrict processing of your data. To exercise these rights, please contact us at privacy@truvideo.co.

8. California Privacy Rights

California residents may have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), including the right to know what personal information is collected, the right to delete, and the right to opt-out of the sale of personal information. TruVideo does not sell personal information.

9. International Data Transfers

TruVideo is based in the United States. If you access our Services from outside the US, your information may be transferred to and processed in the United States, where data protection laws may differ from those in your jurisdiction.

10. Children's Privacy

Our Services are not directed to children under 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 16.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes by posting the updated policy on our website with a revised "Last Updated" date.

Core APIs

Three APIs. One intelligence layer.

Everything horizontal video AI platforms offer — search, analysis, embeddings — plus domain-specific training, integrated capture, and data sovereignty that generic providers can't match.

search Search API

Natural language queries across your entire video library. Find specific moments, objects, spoken words, or on-screen text — without manual tagging or metadata.

POST /v1/search { "query": "technician showing brake pad wear", "index": "automotive-inspections", "options": { "modalities": ["visual", "speech", "text"], "threshold": 0.85, "limit": 20 } }

psychology Analyze API

Extract structured data from video content — damage assessments, compliance checklists, safety violations, parts identification, and workflow state — without writing vision models from scratch.

POST /v1/analyze { "video_id": "insp_8f3a2c", "tasks": [ "damage_assessment", "parts_identification", "compliance_check" ], "model": "automotive-v3" }

hub Embed API

Generate multimodal vector embeddings for any video segment. Power similarity search, clustering, anomaly detection, and recommendation systems with embeddings that understand your domain — not just generic visual features.

POST /v1/embed { "video_id": "insp_8f3a2c", "segments": [{"start": 0, "end": 30}], "model": "multimodal-embed-v2", "dimensions": 1024 }
The Difference

What separates Insight from horizontal video AI

Generic video intelligence platforms build foundation models for everyone. We built an engine that actually knows your industry.

01 — Capture + Understand

Integrated video capture layer

Horizontal platforms require you to capture, encode, and upload video separately. Insight includes TruVideo's capture SDKs — mobile, tablet, fixed camera, body cam — so video flows from lens to intelligence in a single pipeline.

02 — Domain-Trained

80M+ real-world training videos

Generic models learn from YouTube and stock footage. Insight's models are trained on 80 million real inspection, service, and field videos — so they understand brake rotors, turbine blades, roof damage, and fleet wear at expert level.

03 — Workflow-Native

AI results that trigger actions

Other platforms return JSON and leave the rest to you. Insight feeds directly into TruVideo workflows — damage assessment triggers a customer approval request, safety violation generates a compliance ticket, anomaly detection fires a real-time alert.

04 — Predictable Pricing

Per-location, not per-minute

Usage-based pricing means your bill scales with every video you process. Insight uses predictable per-location pricing — process more video, run more queries, train more models. Your cost stays the same.

05 — Real-Time + Recorded

Analyze live streams and archives

Most video AI platforms only process uploaded files. Insight analyzes both real-time streams (RTSP, WebRTC, HLS) and recorded archives — enabling live monitoring, instant alerts, and retrospective search from a single API.

06 — Your Infrastructure

BYOS + BYOAI data sovereignty

Cloud-only platforms force your video onto their servers. Insight runs on your storage (S3, Azure Blob, GCS) with your AI provider (OpenAI, Anthropic, Google, local) — your data never leaves your control.

Multimodal

Four modalities. One query.

Insight processes every information layer in video simultaneously — visual content, speech, on-screen text, and ambient audio — to deliver results that single-modality systems miss entirely.

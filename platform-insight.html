Capabilities

What Insight analyzes

Every call is a data point. Insight turns your phone traffic into structured intelligence — appointment conversion rates, lost opportunity patterns, advisor performance, and customer sentiment.

event_available

Appointment Conversion

Tracks which calls result in booked appointments and which don't. Surfaces the gap between call volume and schedule fill — and identifies the specific conversation patterns that convert at the highest rates.

missed_video_call

Missed Opportunity Detection

Flags calls where a customer expressed service intent but left without booking. Captures the revenue that walked out the door and gives managers the information to recover it.

sentiment_satisfied

Advisor Sentiment Analysis

Scores advisor calls for tone, empathy, urgency handling, and customer satisfaction signals. Coaching recommendations delivered at the call level, not the quarterly review.

school

AI Coaching Signals

Automatically surfaces best-practice examples from your own call history. New advisors learn from your highest-converting calls — not generic training scripts.

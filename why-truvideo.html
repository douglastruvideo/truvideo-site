vs. Fixed Camera Tunnels

Damage detection hardware: $50K+ per location. TruVideo: any phone.

The hardware approach

Fixed camera tunnel systems for dealerships and rental companies cost $50,000+ per installation — plus maintenance, calibration, and ongoing support contracts. They only work for vehicles that drive through the tunnel. They don't work for aircraft, equipment, or field assets. And they detect damage at a point in time — they don't track change over time.

TruVideo's approach

TruVideo Inspect generates a calibrated 3D spatial mesh from a 90-second mobile video walk-around. Any technician, any asset, any location — no hardware installation, no calibration, no $50K upfront cost. The mesh is saved and reloaded at every future inspection, with AR overlays showing previously documented damage.

More importantly: TruVideo proves when damage occurred through change-of-state tracking. Hardware tunnels capture a snapshot. TruVideo creates a timeline.

vs. Legacy Call Analytics

CallRevu & legacy providers: noisy reports. TruVideo: actionable signals.

The legacy problem

Legacy call analytics platforms were built before modern transcription and AI. They have slow transcription pipelines, high false-positive alert rates, and minimal API access. Dealers receive weekly reports that require manual review to surface insights. There's no connection to the broader service workflow — calls are analyzed in isolation.

TruVideo Insight's advantage

TruVideo Insight is built on modern transcription infrastructure (sub-5 second latency, 94% accuracy on shop-floor audio) with AI coaching signals that surface from your own call history — not generic training scripts. And because Insight shares infrastructure with TruVideo's video, messaging, and inspection layers, a call connects to the full service journey.

info About Us

We turn video into proof

TruVideo started with a simple insight: when people can see the evidence, they trust the outcome. From that idea, we've built the visual intelligence platform that 3,500+ businesses rely on to capture, analyze, and act on video data at scale.

Our Story

From dealership innovation to enterprise platform

Founded in 2012 by Joe Shaker and Douglas Chrystall, TruVideo began in automotive service lanes — where a 30-second video of a worn brake pad was more persuasive than a 10-minute phone call. That insight — visual proof beats verbal explanation — turned out to be universal.

Today TruVideo is a full-stack visual intelligence platform serving six industries. Our Video Query Service turns unstructured video into structured, queryable data. Our AI models detect damage, score conditions, and prove when changes occurred. Our messaging engine delivers findings to the right person at the right time.

With 80M+ videos processed and $33M in annual revenue, we've built the largest proprietary dataset of visual asset intelligence in the industry — and we're just getting started.

What We Believe

Our principles

Seeing is proving

When people can see the evidence — the dent, the worn part, the completed repair — trust follows. Every product decision starts with: does this make the invisible visible?

Platform over point solution

We build the intelligence layer, not vertical-specific features. One platform, six industries, infinite applications. Partners integrate once and deploy everywhere.

Your data stays yours

BYOS and BYOAI aren't marketing terms — they're architecture decisions. Video stays in your cloud. AI models can be swapped. TruVideo provides the skill layer, not the lock-in.

Ship fast, learn faster

We operate with a guerrilla mindset — asymmetric bets, rapid iteration, contrarian positions. Speed and conviction beat consensus and polish every time.

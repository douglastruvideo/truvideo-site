Architecture

Why a platform beats point solutions

Point solutions solve one problem in one vertical. A platform solves the same class of problem everywhere — and gets smarter with every deployment.

One SDK. Six industries.

Partners integrate the TruVideo SDK once. Then deploy across automotive, fleet, aviation, field service, insurance, and construction without rebuilding. The intelligence layer adapts — the integration doesn't change.

BYOAI — Bring Your Own AI

Models commoditize. What remains is the skill layer — domain expertise, workflow logic, and compliance frameworks. TruVideo is that skill layer. Swap the model underneath without rewriting your workflows.

80M+ video dataset

Every video processed improves every model. The network effect is real — more customers in more verticals means better intelligence for everyone on the platform.

Enterprise-grade integrations

Pre-built connectors for CDK Global, DealerSocket, Tekion, Reynolds & Reynolds in automotive. Extensible to any DMS, ERP, CMMS, or field service platform via open APIs.

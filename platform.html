Six layers.
One platform.

TruVideo is a full-stack visual intelligence platform — from capture to governance. Every layer compounds the one above it. The result is infrastructure that gets harder to replace with every inspection, every workflow, every deployment.

Layer 1

Capture — Every input, one pipeline

It doesn't matter how video enters the system. Mobile SDK, browser-based recorder, AR smart glasses, or contactless Send2Me links — every frame feeds the same intelligence engine.

smartphone

Mobile SDK

Native iOS and Android SDK drops into any app in days. White-label ready, partner-branded, with offline capture and automatic sync.

language

Web Recorder

Browser-based capture for customers and remote teams — no app install required. Send2Me links let anyone submit video evidence instantly.

visibility

AR Glasses

Hands-free capture with Qualcomm Snapdragon Spaces XR glasses. Walk an aircraft or job site while the system records and analyzes in real time.

cloud_upload

BYOS Architecture

Bring Your Own Storage. Video stays in your cloud — AWS, Azure, GCP, or on-prem. TruVideo processes without ever owning your data.

Layer 2

Media Processing — Prepare every frame for intelligence

Raw video is just pixels. The processing layer transforms it into clean, structured, analysis-ready media — at any scale, in any connectivity environment.

cloud_sync

Adaptive Upload

Chunked, resumable uploads with adaptive compression. Works in 2G shop floors, offline job sites, and aircraft hangar environments.

tune

Transcoding & Playback

Automated transcoding to optimal playback formats. Multi-resolution streaming, thumbnail generation, and CDN delivery — zero configuration.

noise_aware

AI Noise Cancellation

Purpose-built for service environments. Suppresses shop noise, engine sounds, and ambient interference before AI analysis — improving model accuracy by 30%+.

photo_library

Media Library

Searchable, taggable media storage linked to asset records. Videos, images, and documents unified in a single structured repository per asset.

Layer 3

Workflow Integration — Embedded where work happens

Visual intelligence is only valuable if it fits seamlessly into existing workflows. TruVideo integrates at the workflow layer — not as a separate app employees have to remember to use.

integration_instructions

DMS / CRM / ERP Connectors

Pre-built integrations with CDK Global, DealerSocket, Tekion, Reynolds & Reynolds in automotive. Open connector framework for fleet, FSM, ERP, and CMMS platforms.

api

REST APIs & Webhooks

Every platform action is available via API. Webhooks for real-time event streaming — video uploaded, analysis complete, approval granted, message delivered.

admin_panel_settings

Partner Admin Platform

Multi-tenant administration for partners managing thousands of locations. Location management, user provisioning, billing, and configuration at scale.

Layer 4

Intelligence — What makes video actionable

The intelligence layer is where video becomes data. Damage attribution, structured inspection outputs, spatial measurement, and AI-driven decisioning — trained on 80M+ real-world inspection videos.

query_stats

Video Query Service

Query video like a database. VQS transforms unstructured footage into structured, searchable, actionable data — powering every downstream workflow.

view_in_ar

Spatial Measurement & Attribution

Mobile 3D mesh generation with change-of-state tracking. Proves WHEN damage occurred, not just THAT it exists — the core differentiator no competitor has replicated.

history

Asset Memory

Every inspection builds a persistent visual timeline anchored to the asset. The asset remembers — building a growing evidence chain from the first inspection forward.

Layer 5

Communications — Intelligence delivered to the right person

Findings don't have value sitting in a dashboard. The communications layer delivers structured intelligence through compliant, AI-enhanced messaging to customers, technicians, and managers.

chat

Intelligent Messaging

SMS, RCS, and in-app messaging with AI-generated summaries, smart replies, approval workflows, and multi-language support. Two-way conversation with context.

verified_user

Compliance Guard

Enterprise-grade compliance engine with hard blocks (profanity, competitor mentions, PII) and soft warnings. Every message logged, tracked, and audit-ready.

smart_toy

AI Agents

Conversational AI across web, chat, and phone for appointment scheduling, service FAQs, and intelligent escalation to human staff with full context.

Layer 6

Reporting & Governance — Proof the platform is working

Enterprise deployments require accountability. The governance layer surfaces the analytics, compliance records, and quality metrics that demonstrate ROI — and make TruVideo auditable at every level.

bar_chart

Quality Dashboards

Video Quality Score (VQS) analytics across every location, technician, and workflow. Identify coaching opportunities and track improvement over time — at scale.

policy

Compliance & Auditability

Tamper-evident records with complete audit trails. Every video timestamped, geotagged, and linked to the operator who captured it — FMCSA, OSHA, and enterprise compliance ready.

hub

Partner & Enterprise Controls

Multi-tenant billing, location-level configuration, enterprise SSO, and role-based access. Partners manage thousands of locations; enterprises control every deployment parameter.

Architecture

Why a platform beats point solutions

Point solutions solve one problem in one vertical. A platform solves the same class of problem everywhere — and gets smarter with every deployment.

One SDK. Six industries.

Partners integrate the TruVideo SDK once. Then deploy across automotive, fleet, aviation, field service, insurance, and construction without rebuilding. The intelligence layer adapts — the integration doesn't change.

BYOAI — Bring Your Own AI

Models commoditize. What remains is the skill layer — domain expertise, workflow logic, and compliance frameworks. TruVideo is that skill layer. Swap the model underneath without rewriting your workflows.

80M+ video dataset

Every video processed improves every model. The network effect is real — more customers in more verticals means better intelligence for everyone on the platform.

Enterprise-grade integrations

Pre-built connectors for CDK Global, DealerSocket, Tekion, Reynolds & Reynolds in automotive. Extensible to any DMS, ERP, CMMS, or field service platform via open APIs.

Capabilities

Marketing that knows your customer's vehicle

Generic CRM campaigns send the same message to everyone. Reach triggers campaigns from real service data — inspection findings, mileage milestones, unaccepted recommendations, and seasonal patterns.

search

Inspection-Triggered Campaigns

When a technician documents a worn brake pad or low tire tread, Reach can automatically schedule a follow-up message to the customer. Marketing from real findings, not generic service intervals.

replay

Declined Service Recovery

Customers who declined a service recommendation receive a timed follow-up sequence — with the original video evidence attached. Recovery rates increase when customers see the actual condition documentation.

timeline

Lifecycle & Milestone Campaigns

Mileage-based, season-based, and model-specific campaigns triggered automatically from your DMS data. Oil changes, tire rotations, recall notifications — delivered at the right moment.

Multi-Channel Delivery

SMS, RCS, email, and in-app — delivered through TruVideo Connect's compliance-grade messaging infrastructure. Every message is logged, tracked, and compliant.
